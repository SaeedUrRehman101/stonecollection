COUTURE

Couture is synonymous with design, craft, and exquisite beauty. The collection is carefully handcrafted and each piece is a graceful fusion of elegant classic design and a modern aesthetic. Emeralds, sapphires, carved jades, and onyxes are set with white diamonds to create a sharp luminosity. Each gemstone’s shape, color and radiance guide the design process and the result is an eclectic and distinctively luxurious collection of statement earrings, necklaces, and rings.
